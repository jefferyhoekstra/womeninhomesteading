What is Women in Homesteading?

Women in Homesteading is an in-person fellowship group of women dedicated to keeping the old ways alive. We believe in Community Sufficiency. We believe basic skills should be shared freely. Women in Homesteading is meant to empower, connect and inspire women. Women who desire to explore and/or join the Homesteading community. As well as women who desire to advance their skillsets, form relationships with other like-minded women, and build community.
